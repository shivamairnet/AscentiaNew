Design Command Center Our Dynamic Admin Panel For Interior Innovation

Our adaptable admin panel provides crucial interior design features geared to your project management needs, including resource coordination, progress tracking, performance metrics analysis and more.

Role Based Access
  • With role-based access features, business administrators can create different user roles within the system, such as designers, project managers, clients, and contractors, each with their own set of rights and access levels.
  • Administrators can also use access control lists to specify which resources and data each user role can access within the system. This comprises design files, project documentation, client data, and communication routes.
  • Administrators can set up a hierarchical user access control structure, with higher-level roles having access to more general functionality and lower-level roles having access to more particular activities and features.
Resource Management
  • With resource management, administrators can keep a consolidated inventory of design resources such as furniture, materials, finishes, accessories, and equipment.
  • Admins can develop and maintain design resource catalogs, which include adding new design items, changing item details.
  • Integration of resource management and procurement technologies enables administrators to source and purchase design resources from vendors, including making purchase orders, tracking orders and delivery, managing invoices and payments.
Document Management
  • Administrators can store and manage comprehensive client profiles, which include contact details, interaction history, preferences, and notes.
  • Our system also allows administrators to tag documents with keywords and metadata such as project name, client name, date, and document type, allowing for faster search and retrieval of relevant documents.
  • Admins can also make document sharing and collaboration easier for customers and stakeholders by allowing users to securely exchange documents, collaborate on document editing, and follow document changes in real-time.
Data Analysis & Reporting
  • With reporting and analytics, our system provides interactive analysis dashboards that visually depict data through charts, graphs, and tables, allowing administrators to quickly identify trends, patterns, and outliers, facilitating data-driven decision-making.
  • Our systems admin panel supports real-time analytics, allowing administrators to quickly monitor project performance, track changes, and respond to developing patterns.
  • Our system admin interface also allows administrators to delve deep down into individual data points or categories within reports to acquire a better understanding of the underlying design aspects and trends.
